HOW TO INSTALL
1) open up google chrome
2) go to settings, then extensions
3) turn on developer mode in the top right corner
4) click on load unpacked extension
5) find the source of this folder and upload the folder * do not upload an individual file, it will not work*
6) restart browser and use at ease!
